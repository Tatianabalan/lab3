Image by Pexels.

Bees play a very important role in pollinating plants. Often times, the importance of these bees is overlooked and not a lot of people are aware of their importance. Without bees, our food system would collapse. Learn more about bees and their importance so we can do our best to make a difference to ensure their survival.
